About Us

Assisting Families through Compassionate Support

Established in 2005, Essential Autism provides educational support and services to people of all ages who have a developmental disability. We specialize in Behaviour Analysis, delivering services to families and service providers in Windsor-Essex and London Ontario. Our skilled team provides supports in-home and in-community, based on the specific needs of the individual.

Our Services

Personalized Care Tailored to Each Individual's Needs

Behavioural Therapy

Essential Autism offers specialized Behavioural Analysis (BA) therapy focusing on improving behaviours and building skills crucial for daily life, such as communication and social skills.

Respite Services

We offer respite services, providing families with valuable relief from caregiving responsibilities and ensuring that their loved ones receive dedicated, compassionate care in a supportive and safe environment.

Training and Public Speaking

We provide expert training and public speaking services designed to empower individuals and organizations with practical strategies for understanding and supporting individuals with developmental disabilities.

Ask By Client

Frequently Asked Questions

Explore answers to your questions about autism support, educational services, and our commitment to families.

What types of support services do you offer?

We specialize in providing Behaviour Analysis (BA) therapy and Respite Services. Our BA therapy programs are tailored to promote positive behaviour changes and skill development in individuals with individuals with developmental disabilities. Additionally, we offer respite services to support families by providing temporary relief and care for their loved ones as well as training and public speaking.

How can I begin services with Essential Autism?

Fill out our contact form with your information, and our team will reach out to you promptly to schedule a consultation or meeting.

What age groups do you provide services for?

Essential Autism offers services for individuals of all ages, from early childhood through adulthood.
