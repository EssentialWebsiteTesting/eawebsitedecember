Frequently Asked Questions

Explore answers to your questions about autism support, educational services, and our commitment to families.

What types of support services do you offer?

We specialize in providing Applied Behaviour Analysis (ABA) and Respite Services. Our ABA programs are tailored to promote positive behaviour changes and skill development in individuals with developmental disabilities.

How can I begin services with Essential Autism?

Fill out our contact form with your information and our team will reach out to you promptly to schedule a consultation or meeting.

What age groups do you provide services for?

Essential Autism offers services for individuals of all ages, from early childhood through adulthood.

Are your services covered by insurance?

We accept various forms of funding, including OAP, Passport, and Respite funds, among others. We are also listed as an approved OAP provider. With Dwayne serving as our Clinical Supervisor, we can assist families in accessing OAP funding for Applied Behaviour Analysis services. Additionally, many insurance plans cover our services. We encourage you to contact your insurance provider to confirm your specific coverage details.

How can I prepare my child for their first ABA session?

Preparing for the first session can help your child feel more comfortable. You can talk to them about what to expect, bring any items they find comforting, and familiarize yourself with our team by visiting the "Our Team" section on our website. This can help you and your child feel more confident about who will be working with them.

How do I know if my child needs ABA?

If you're concerned about your child's communication, social interaction, or behaviour, we recommend reaching out to local healthcare professionals or support services for guidance. They can help determine whether ABA therapy might be beneficial.

What qualifications do your therapists have?

Our therapists are thoroughly trained in Applied Behaviour Analysis, ensuring quality care for your loved ones. While not all team members have formal psychology or behavioural backgrounds, they each bring unique experiences and skills to their roles, fostering a smooth and supportive environment.

Do you offer virtual services?

Yes, we provide virtual therapy sessions to accommodate families' needs and preferences.

How often will my child receive therapy?

The frequency of therapy sessions is customized to meet each individual's unique needs, as determined during the initial consultation.

What should I expect during the first session?

The first session typically involves assessments, goal-setting, and creating a personalized treatment plan for your child.
