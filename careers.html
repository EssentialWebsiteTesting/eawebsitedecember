Why Join Essential Autism?

At Essential Autism, every team member makes a difference. Our staff and volunteers are at the heart of the services we provide, supporting families and individuals with autism in meaningful ways.

Whether you’re interested in gaining experience as a Behavioural Technician, contributing as a Volunteer, or joining our skilled Respite team, your commitment matters. We offer a supportive environment where your skills are valued, and your contributions help us grow as a community-focused organization.

As of right now, for Behavioural Technicians and Respite Workers, Monday and Wednesday 3–8 PM shifts are mandatory. These positions are currently hiring, so if you meet the availability requirements, we encourage you to apply.

When you join our team, you can expect:

- Hands-on experience supporting individuals with autism
- Professional development and training opportunities
- Collaborative and inclusive work environment
- Chance to make a meaningful impact in your community
- A team built on family, support, and collaboration
