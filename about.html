About Us

Supporting Families through Compassionate Education

Established in 2005, Essential Autism provides educational support and services to people of all ages who have a developmental disability. We specialize in Behaviour Analysis, delivering services to families and service providers in Windsor-Essex and London, Ontario. Our skilled team provides supports in-home and in-community, based on the specific needs of the individual.

Our Mission

What Drives Us!

Essential Autism is committed to unwavering determination and relentless support for all individuals, ensuring no one is left behind. Beyond autism, we extend our services to diverse communities, embodying inclusivity and empathy in every interaction. Our values of compassion, perseverance, and personalized care drive us to empower every individual, celebrating their unique strengths and achievements on their journey to an improved quality of life.

At Essential Autism, we believe that every individual deserves the opportunity to reach their full potential. Our mission is driven by the following core principles:

  • Empowerment: We empower individuals and families by providing them with the resources, knowledge, and support they need to navigate the challenges they face.
  • Collaboration: We work collaboratively with families, schools, and community organizations to ensure a holistic approach to support that meets the diverse needs of each individual.
  • Inclusion: We are dedicated to creating an inclusive environment where everyone feels valued and supported, fostering a sense of belonging for all.
  • Continuous Improvement: We are committed to ongoing training and professional development for our staff, ensuring they are equipped with the latest knowledge and best practices in the field of autism support.
  • Advocacy: We advocate for the rights and needs of individuals with autism and their families, raising awareness and promoting understanding within the community.

Through these guiding principles, Essential Autism strives to make a meaningful impact in the lives of those we serve, helping them achieve a higher quality of life and a brighter future.

Our Story

Where We Started!

Dwayne began his journey as a special education teacher, launching the business from his home. Through dedication and hard work, he has successfully expanded its reach, and he remains committed to continuing this growth.

Our story continues to evolve as we adapt to the needs of our clients and the community. We have embraced innovation, enhancing our services and approaches to better support individuals with developmental disabilities. Key objectives in our journey include:

  • Geographic Expansion: Our goal is to extend our services to even more regions across Ontario, ensuring that families everywhere have access to the support they need.
  • Family Business Culture: As we grow, we remain committed to preserving the close-knit, family-oriented atmosphere that defines our organization, ensuring that every team member feels valued and connected.
  • Journey of Growth: Dwayne’s transformation from a special education teacher and professor at Oakland University to the driving force behind Essential Autism illustrates how far we have come, leading the business with passion and dedication.

As we look to the future, our mission remains clear: to empower individuals and families through compassionate support and to make a meaningful impact in the community.

About our Experts

Behaviour Analyst (BAs)

Our behaviour analysis professionals at EA are experts in designing personalized therapy plans and conducting assessments. They provide ongoing support to ensure effective treatment outcomes for all clients.

Commitment
Designing personalized, evidence-based therapy plans to achieve meaningful outcomes.

Administrators

Our administrative team at EA manages daily operations, client communications, and scheduling. They ensure smooth coordination among families, therapists, and service providers, always available to assist.

Commitment
Ensuring seamless communication and support for families and staff.

Technicians

Our technicians at EA are compassionate professionals who implement plans from our BAs. They provide hands-on support, playing a key role in delivering personalized care and positive outcomes.

Commitment
Delivering consistent, compassionate care to help clients reach their goals.

Steps To Finding The Perfect Match

At Essential Autism, we follow a 3-step process, known as the 3-M's, to find the perfect match for each family.

MEET

Begin with a personal meeting to assess individual needs and design tailored therapy plans.

MATCH

Build a meaningful connection between individuals and their support workers, fostering trust for effective collaboration.

MEMORIES

Create lasting memories by celebrating progress and fostering positive experiences for individuals and families.
