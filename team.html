Meet The Founder and CEO


Dwayne Wessel

Dwayne Wessel is the CEO and Clinical Director of Essential Autism, a position he has held since the organization's establishment in 2005. Under his leadership, Essential Autism has become a prominent provider of educational support services for individuals with developmental disabilities, specializing in personalized approaches based on Behaviour Analysis (BA).

Wessel is not only dedicated to delivering high-quality services but also plays a significant role in community education. He frequently presents workshops across Canada and the U.S., focusing on critical topics such as autism awareness, communication strategies, and fostering greater independence for individuals with autism. His commitment to improving the lives of individuals with developmental disabilities and their families is evident through both the services offered by Essential Autism and his outreach efforts.
