The Behavioural Approach

Behaviour Analysis

At Essential Autism, our Applied Behaviour Analysis (ABA) services are delivered through a close collaborative effort between Behaviour Analysts (BAs) and dedicated technicians. But what exactly is ABA? It’s a scientifically validated method that focuses on improving specific behaviours while teaching new skills. Our team emphasizes the importance of data collection using our new data collection system called Hi Rasmus, utilizing real-time information to track progress and adjust strategies to ensure optimal outcomes for each child. This individualized approach allows us to tailor interventions to meet the unique needs of every child, fostering skill development and enhancing their quality of life.

Support for Families

Respite Services

Understanding the challenges families face, Essential Autism offers comprehensive respite services designed to provide caregivers with a much-needed break. But what is respite? It is a temporary relief that allows families to recharge while ensuring that their loved ones receive quality care. We offer respite in both home and office settings, allowing families to choose what suits them best. Our trained technicians provide compassionate support, creating a safe environment where individuals can thrive. This unique combination of flexibility and understanding sets our respite services apart, making a positive impact on families’ overall well-being.

Fostering Inclusivity

Community Involvement

Essential Autism actively engages with the community, creating inclusive environments for children with developmental disabilities to connect and socialize. Our weekly gaming groups, featuring popular platforms like Minecraft and Roblox, provide opportunities for children to interact with peers in a fun and supportive setting. Additionally, we host an in-person game night where children can play board games and engage in face-to-face interactions. Led by our trained technicians, these activities allow children to build friendships while developing essential social skills. By fostering these connections, Essential Autism stands out as an organization dedicated to not just therapy, but also creating a sense of community and belonging.
